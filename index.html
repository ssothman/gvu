Gaza Virtual University

All of Gaza’s university campuses have been affected significantly, with many members of the academic community facing unprecedented challenges. GVU represents a collective effort to support and advance higher learning in Palestine through international collaboration.

  • Learn more

Vision

Gaza Virtual University (GVU) supports the vitality of higher education institutions in Palestine through steadfast international collaboration and innovation.

  • Learn more

Mission

Gaza Virtual University is dedicated to developing and operating a network of international academic partners to ensure the continuity and progress of higher education in Palestine. Our mission encompasses a wide range of supportive activities aimed at addressing the immediate and long-term needs of the academic community in Gaza and beyond.

  • Learn more

How to Help

GVU offers numerous avenues for individuals and institutions worldwide to contribute to the resilience and growth of higher education in Palestine. Whether through academic collaboration, financial support, or volunteer work, your contribution can make a significant difference.

What we do

GVU aims to support indigenous Palestinian institutions and therefore does not offer certificate or degree programs directly to learners. .

Amplifying Urgent Needs

Operates an information clearinghouse on the status of urgent needs identified by Gaza’s universities as they resume operation.

Fostering Global Support

Solicits international academic institutions and individual academics for teaching, research, and administrative support.

Creating Opportunities

Connects professors in Gaza with paid teaching, research, and consulting opportunities in the network.

Empowering Volunteer Faculty

Recruits and appoints international academics to serve as volunteer GVU “faculty,” mirroring a university structure with departments and schools.

Securing Future Growth

Coordinates teams of GVU faculty who seek funding (e.g., project proposals, donations) on behalf of Palestinian (with special focus on Gaza initially) universities that request such assistance, and serves as an information clearinghouse on philanthropic or other funding opportunities for Palestinian universities.

Bridging Global Student Networks

Connects students from around the world with students in Palestine through activities such as tutoring, peer advising, hackathons, global design challenges (not an immediate goal).
